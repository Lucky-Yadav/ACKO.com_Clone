Can You Have Two Insurance Policies on the Same Car?

Team Acko • May 24, 2022

Car insurance is essential for financial protection against third-party liabilities and damages to your car. But can you have two insurance policies on the same car? This article has the answer. Read ahead and make the right policy purchase decision.

Content

Is it illegal to have double insurance policies for the same vehicle in India?

Can a vehicle be insured twice? While there is no legal limitation on buying double insurance for a vehicle in India, it is advised to avoid two insurance policies for one car. If you buy two policies for a car, it will cost you double the price. It can also be an issue during claim settlement.

Should you insure one car from two different companies at the same time?

Ideally, you can raise a claim and acquire compensation only from one insurer at a time. For instance, if your car gets damaged in an accident, you can’t raise claims for the same event with two insurers and receive the entire settlement amount twice. Thus, there is no point in purchasing two policies when you can claim only one at a time. Let’s talk further about the disadvantages of double insurance for one car.

Disadvantages of double car insurance

As mentioned above, double car insurance doesn’t add more value in terms of coverage. In fact, it makes you pay more car insurance premium, which doesn’t seem economically viable.

Here are the key disadvantages of buying double car insurance.

  • Two policies may cause unnecessary confusion.
  • Even with double policy coverage for a car, ideally, you can claim compensation only from one.
  • Not to mention that you will be paying an additional premium for no added value.

How can you avoid buying two insurance policies for a car?

You may end up with policy overlap in case you renew your policy before the expiration of an existing insurance plan and begin the new policy immediately. To avoid such a situation, you must thoroughly read the policy documents and check the expiration date of your existing car insurance before renewing with a different company. Note that these scenarios are hypothetical and used for explanation purposes only.

Is insurance on multiple vehicles possible?

Such a plan is subject to availability. However, for explanation purposes, here’s how it can work. If you own two or more motor vehicles, it is possible to insure them together with a special type of policy. Such an insurance policy is called a motor floater policy which may include Own Damage (OD) cover or Comprehensive cover (Including OD and Third-party car insurance)

Can I buy two policies for two different cars?

It is completely legal to purchase two insurance plans for two different four-wheelers. You can choose to buy different policies from the same or different insurance companies, depending on your needs. How do insurance companies identify double insurance frauds?

As you already know, there might be situations when you purchase double insurance for one car by mistake. Hence, the insurance companies evaluate the cases based on certain criteria to identify insurance frauds over genuine mistakes.

Here are the parameters used by the insurers to identify double insurance frauds.

  • Whether or not a policyholder obtained benefits at the insurer’s expense.
  • Whether or not a policyholder had awareness of the claim-payment benefits they received using double insurance.
  • Whether or not a policyholder had kept the benefits even after being aware of the profit of claims on double insurance.

Final thoughts

So, can a car be insured twice? In a nutshell, buying insurance policies from two companies at the same time, for the same car isn’t illegal. But it can definitely lead to paying excessive premiums and cause confusion during claims. In fact, ideally, you won’t be able to raise two claims for the same event related to your car. So, it would be wise to simply avoid double insurance and purchase a Comprehensive Policy that can cover your car in different scenarios.

Frequently asked questions

Here are a few common questions answered about buying insurance for your car in India.

Should two drivers buy insurance for the same vehicle?

Only one car insurance plan should be purchased for a vehicle.

Is there a limit to the number of claims a policyholder can raise per year?

There is no limitation on the number of claims you can raise on vehicle insurance annually. However, it is better to avoid raising too many claims against a Comprehensive Policy to protect your NCB (No Claim Bonus), which helps to get a discount on the premium at the time of renewal.

Which insurance plan would be best suited for my car?

Rather than buying double insurance from different companies, you can choose a Comprehensive Car Insurance Plan. A Comprehensive Insurance Plan covers a wide range of situations related to damage, loss and theft. Moreover, you can purchase car insurance add-ons to further extend the coverage for your car.
